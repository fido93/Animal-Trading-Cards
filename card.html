Munchkin Cats

With its short little legs and low-slung torso, some people view the Munchkin cat as the feline version of a Dachshund

  • Accidental Discovery: The Wizard of Oz
  • Have a Seat: short-legged cat in Russia was named the “Stalingrad kangaroo cat”
  • Not All Munchkins are Alike: There are actually three different types of leg lengths in the munchkin breed
  • Just A Typical Cat: munchkin cat does not suffer from spinal issues due to their short legs.
